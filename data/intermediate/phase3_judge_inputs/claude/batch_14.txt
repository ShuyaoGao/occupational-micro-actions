EXPERT REVIEW -- Micro-Action Sequence Evaluation
For each task, read ALL four options, then choose the BEST (A/B/C/D).

Criteria:
  1. Completeness  -- no critical steps missing
  2. Realism       -- every step is concretely executable
  3. Stage accuracy -- stage label correct for each step:
     IC=Intent_Communication  NA=Navigation_Addressing
     PD=Perception_Diagnosis  ME=Manipulation_Execution  FV=Feedback_Verification
  4. Conciseness   -- no redundant or duplicate steps
  5. Scope         -- starts and ends at exactly the right point

OUTPUT: fill the ANSWER TEMPLATE at the bottom.
Format: N: X | one-sentence reason (max 12 words)
IMPORTANT: Each task has a different best answer. Do NOT pick the same
letter for every task without reading.

  7. [ME] Begin cutting along the marked line, maintaining a consistent speed and torch angle to ensure a straight, even cut.
  8. [PD] Monitor the cut's progress, adjusting the torch angle and speed as needed to maintain a clean, straight edge.
  9. [FV] Once the cut is complete, allow the plate to cool naturally before removing the slag and deburring the edges using a wire brush or grinder.
  10. [FV] Inspect the cut for any imperfections, ensuring it meets the required quality standards.
  11. [FV] Document the completed work, including the cut dimensions, any issues encountered, and the final quality assessment, for the work order records.
