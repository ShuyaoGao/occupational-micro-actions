--- Task 113: Operate cash registers.
[A]
  1. [IC] Greet the customer and prepare the cash register for the transaction.
  2. [PD] Scan the barcode of each item using the handheld scanner or enter the product code manually for items without barcodes.

  4. [ME] Calculate the total cost of the purchase and display it on the register screen.
  5. [IC] Ask the customer how they would like to pay and process the payment (cash, card, or mobile).
  6. [FV] Print a receipt and hand it to the customer if requested.

  8. [ME] Print the receipt and hand it to the customer.
